Title: ICICI Savings Account Opening (General Procedure)

Online:
1. Go to ICICI Bank official website or iMobile app.
2. Enter mobile number, PAN, and basic details.
3. Verify OTP sent to mobile number.
4. Complete KYC (e-KYC / Video KYC if required).
5. Submit details and receive account confirmation.

Offline (Branch):
1. Visit ICICI branch with PAN and address proof.
2. Fill account opening form and complete KYC.
3. Deposit initial amount (if required).
4. Account is opened and details are shared.
Security Note:
- Do not share OTP, PIN, CVV, or passwords in chat.
- For personal account details, user must log in.
